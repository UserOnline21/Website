Abyssus Review.

Home

Abyssus is a roguelike first person shooter game, with upgrades to buy, weapons to find and secrets to discover. Abyssus is set in an abandoned underwater temple, stranded and forced to rely on a strange power to survive.

the game is cut into three zones, each zone having unique enemies, an elite at the halfway point, and a boss at the end. defeating elite and boss enemies gives you soul orbs, allowing you to buy permanant upgrades.

the weapon system in abyssus is incredible. each weapon has 2 parts that can be edited, each part has 3 attachments to pick from. to unlock these attachments you must do challenges with the weapon equipped. there are also gadgets which are used for utility.
