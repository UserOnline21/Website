Noita Review

Home

Noita is a roguelike game physics simulator where every pixel is simulated. this means:

  • everything can be destroyed down to a single pixel
  • powders like sand and snow flow naturally
  • every single pixel is trying to kill you.

Noita is a very hard game, see below.

the difficulty of the game is balanced by exploration and learning. each death teaches you something new to use in the next run.

Noita is filled with mysteries to uncover. and while the game may break you, you are given the tools to break it back.

the tools to break Noita come in the form of wands and spells, tinkering with your wand is an integral part of Noita. with something as simple as a homing rock being one of the most powerful spells in the game.

Noita has more than just spells. It also has alchemy in the form of magical liquids and powders that react and change when mixed. dilute poison with water, transform solid steel into steam and make gold out of nothing!

Noita above all else is a game that tests you with unrivalved challenge and adversary, with a world that extends into infinite possibilities, beating the game is akin to beating the tutorial as the world opens up to you as you learn what you are capable of. your wands and spells becoming so powerful that the biggest threat to your run is your own PC giving out on you. starting as a simple mage in Noita, you finally surpass the gods themselves.. and then a polymorph potion results in your death, i highly reccomend Noita to anyone that enjoys roguelikes, magic and brutally hard games that reward you for learning to exploit the system!
