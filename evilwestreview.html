Evil West is a beat-em-up action adventure game set in the old west. you fight against monsters, vampires and more, getting new upgrades and perks as you play.

Evil West can be played alone or with a friend. multiplayer is tied to the host player's progress so even if you jump in mid-way through you won't have to catch up.

immerse yourself in the world with hundreds of unique enemies to fight, all with lore documents to learn about your foes and feel like a true vampire hunting cowboy! each level has optional lore documents to find to give extra information on the world of Evil West.

evil west is a game with an amazing story, fantastic gameplay, and great accessability with its difficulty options, if you ever want to feel like a rooting, tooting, gun shooting cowboy taking on the dark forces of the night, evil west is the game for you.
